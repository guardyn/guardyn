‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my sponsorship anytime?

Yes! GitHub Sponsors and Open Collective allow you to cancel anytime with no penalties. We appreciate any support, whether for one month or many years.

Is my sponsorship tax-deductible?

If you sponsor through Open Collective, your contribution may be tax-deductible (depending on your country and tax laws). GitHub Sponsors payments are generally not tax-deductible. Consult your tax advisor for specific guidance.

Will Guardyn always be free and open source?

Yes! The core Guardyn platform will always be 100% free, open source (Apache 2.0 license), and self-hostable. We will offer optional paid services (managed hosting, enterprise support) to sustain development, but the code will remain free forever.

How are sponsors recognized?

All sponsors are listed in our README.md on GitHub and on this sponsors page. Higher tiers receive logo placement on the homepage and additional recognition. You can choose to remain anonymous if you prefer.

Can I sponsor as a company?

Absolutely! Corporate sponsorships are welcome and come with additional benefits like logo placement, priority support, and influence on the roadmap. For Enterprise Partner inquiries ($2,000+/month), please contact enterprise@guardyn.app.

What happens if you don't reach your funding goals?

If we don't reach sustainable funding ($2,000-5,000/month), development will slow down as the founder will need to take on part-time consulting work. However, the project will continue‚Äîjust at a slower pace. Your sponsorship directly accelerates development!
